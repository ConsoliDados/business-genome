# Channel format rules

> **Stub** — format/length conventions per channel. `draft-post` follows these.

- **blog** — long-form, structured, SEO-aware; the deep/destination piece.
- **linkedin** — short/medium hook-first; the audience edge that derives elsewhere.
- **substack** — long-form essay / newsletter; promotion destination.
- **x** — short / thread; punchy.
- **instagram** — visual-first; short caption.
- **youtube** — `content-type: video-script` (parked): hook → segments → CTA.
