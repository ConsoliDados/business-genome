# Business plan — {{Name}}

> Generalized from the ConsoliDados **SEBRAE "Plano de Negócios"** model. For client work this
> may be a summarized version; for internal products it's the full plan. Pull market/persona/
> feasibility in by link — don't restate. Mark gaps **ASK-CLIENT / ASK-OWNER**.

## Company / product overview
> One paragraph "what we do" + origin/context.

## 1. Executive summary
> The whole plan in one page — write last, read first.
- **Key highlights / thesis** (3–5 bullets)
- **How we operate** (operating model, value levers)
- **Products & services** (niches/lines → anchor products)
- **Target customers** (segments, ICP) → see [[personas]]
- **Capital to invest** — phased:

| Stage | Period | Amount | Deliverables |
|-------|--------|--------|--------------|

- **Projected monthly revenue** (per line; assumptions: GMV, margin, commission)
- **Target profit** (gross profit, margin %, growth assumptions)

## 2. Founders & equity *(internal product)*
- Profiles (name, role, experience) · pro-labore % · profit-split %.

## 3. Strategic identity
- Mission · Vision · Values.

## 4. Market analysis
- Customer study (characteristics, behavior, purchase drivers, where they are).
- Sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM) → see [[market]]. Competitors & suppliers.

## 5. Marketing plan
- Products & services description · pricing model · value proposition · promotional strategy ·
  **personas** → [[personas]] · sales/commercialization structure · channels.

## 6. Operational plan
- Capacity (service/productive) · operational processes · staffing needs.

## 7. Financial plan
- Investments (CapEx + pre-operating), phased → **total investment**.
- Working capital · monthly revenue forecast (ramp → steady-state) · costs (COGS, sales/
  commissions, labor, depreciation, fixed monthly).
- **Income statement (DRE / P&L)**: revenue → −COGS → gross → −OpEx → EBITDA → net.
- **Viability indicators**: break-even · profitability · ROI/rentability · payback. See [[finance]].

## 8. Funding & valuation *(if raising)*
- Funding sources, capital structure, runway, use of proceeds.
- Valuation: objective factors (cash flow, assets, debt, sales) + subjective (market size,
  barriers, moat, innovation); method (asset-based / replacement / VC method w/ P/E multiple).

## 9. Scenarios
- Best / base / worst case.

## 10. Strategic assessment
- SWOT · Porter's Five Forces · BCG matrix.

## 11. Plan evaluation & data-gathering
- Go/no-go against the plan's own assumptions · checklist of data still needed.
