# Feasibility study — {{Name}}

> Derived from the ConsoliDados business-studies model (SEBRAE-based). Fill from real inputs;
> mark unknowns **ASK-CLIENT / ASK-OWNER**. Put the recommendation up front.

## 1. Executive summary
- Problem + proposed solution (1 paragraph).
- **Recommendation: GO / NO-GO / CONDITIONAL** + 3–5 key drivers.

## 2. Scope & assumptions
- In / out of scope.
- Core assumptions (demand, pricing, cost, timeline) — tag each H/M/L confidence.
- Constraints (budget, deadline, regulatory, headcount).

## 3. Market / demand feasibility
- Segment & size (TAM/SAM/SOM) → see [[market]].
- Demand evidence (pipeline, LOIs, benchmarks); competition & barriers to entry.

## 4. Technical feasibility
- Approach / architecture; build vs buy vs partner.
- Tech, integrations, data, infra; key technical risks + mitigations; dependencies & critical path.

## 5. Operational feasibility
- Process / capacity fit; staffing & skills (have vs need); partners/vendors; scalability.

## 6. Economic / financial feasibility
- Total investment (phased), working capital, revenue model, cost structure.
- **Viability indicators** (see [[finance]]): break-even · profitability (net margin) ·
  ROI / rentability · payback period · (NPV / IRR if multi-year).

## 7. Scenario analysis
- Pessimistic / base / optimistic; sensitivity — which variable breaks the case?

## 8. Strategic assessment
- SWOT · Porter's Five Forces · BCG (where relevant).

## 9. Risk register
| Risk | Type (tech/market/ops/financial/legal) | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation | Owner |
|------|----------------------------------------|:----------:|:------:|------------|-------|

## 10. Go / no-go decision
- Decision criteria & thresholds · scorecard per dimension (pass/fail/conditional) ·
  **verdict + conditions to proceed** · next steps & decision date.
