# Skill — board-cards

> **CLI:** run to break the roadmap "Now" into Kanban cards. **Web-chat:** paste the prompt.

**Writes:** `…/delivery/board.md` (To do / Doing / Done / Archive)

## Steps
1. Turn each roadmap "Now" item into one or more actionable cards under **To do**.
2. Keep cards small and verb-first. The human moves cards across columns.

## Guard-rails
- You don't archive cards — that's a manual Kanban action by the human.

---
## Prompt (copy-paste)
> Break these "Now" roadmap items into small, verb-first Kanban cards for the To do column.
