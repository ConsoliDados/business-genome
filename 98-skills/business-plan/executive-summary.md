# Skill — executive-summary

> Chapter of [[README|business-plan]]. **Write this LAST** — it synthesizes the finished chapters.
> **CLI:** run after the other chapters exist. **Web-chat:** paste.

**Writes:** the *Executive summary* section of `…/strategy/business-plan.md`

## Steps
1. One page max. Pull from the written chapters (don't invent beyond them):
   - **Thesis** (3–5 bullets) · **how we operate** · **products & target customers**
   - **Capital to invest** (phased total) · **projected revenue** · **target profit / margin**
2. Lead with the single most compelling line. End with the ask (if raising).

## Guard-rails
- It must be readable standalone and consistent with every chapter. Written last, read first.
