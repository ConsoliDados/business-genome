# Skill — operational-plan

> Chapter of [[README|business-plan]]. **CLI:** run for the operations chapter. **Web-chat:** paste.

**Writes:** the *Operational plan* section of `…/strategy/business-plan.md`

## Steps
1. **Capacity** — productive/service capacity and its limits.
2. **Operational processes** — the core delivery workflow end to end.
3. **Staffing needs** — roles, levels, when each is needed (link [[13-company/people/README|people]]).
4. **Layout / footprint** (if physical).

## Guard-rails
- Tie capacity to the revenue forecast — don't promise throughput the team can't deliver.

---
## Prompt (copy-paste)
> Write the operational plan for <product/service>: delivery capacity & limits, the core
> operational processes, staffing needs (roles/levels/timing), and footprint if relevant. Keep
> capacity consistent with the revenue forecast.
