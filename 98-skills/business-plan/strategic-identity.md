# Skill — strategic-identity

> Chapter of [[README|business-plan]]. **CLI:** run for the identity chapter. **Web-chat:** paste.

**Writes:** the *Strategic identity* section of `…/strategy/business-plan.md`

## Steps
1. **Mission** — why the 13-company/product exists (present tense, concrete).
2. **Vision** — where it's going (3–5 yr north star).
3. **Values** — 3–6 operating principles, each with a one-line "what it means in practice".
4. **Positioning statement** — For *<target>* who *<need>*, *<product>* is a *<category>* that
   *<key benefit>*, unlike *<alt>*, because *<differentiator>*.

## Guard-rails
- No platitudes. Each value must change a decision. Positioning must name a real alternative.
