# Skill — funding-valuation

> Chapter of [[README|business-plan]]. Use **only if raising / valuing**. **CLI:** run for the
> funding chapter. **Web-chat:** paste.

**Writes:** the *Funding & valuation* section of `…/strategy/business-plan.md`

## Steps
1. **Funding sources** — founder capital, angel, VC, debt, grants.
2. **Capital structure** — equity split, instruments.
3. **Runway** = cash ÷ net monthly burn; **use of proceeds**.
4. **Valuation**:
   - Objective factors: cash flow, assets/equity, debt, sales volume.
   - Subjective factors: market size, entry barriers, moat, innovation, value prop, intangibles.
   - Methods: asset-based · replacement (base-zero) · **VC method** (investment, projected exit-
     year revenue, expected net margin, P/E multiple → exit valuation → present value).

## Guard-rails
- Valuation is a range with assumptions, not a single magic number. Show the method.

## Prompt (copy-paste)
> Draft funding & valuation for <venture>: funding sources, capital structure, runway, use of
> proceeds, and a valuation using the VC method (state inputs: investment, exit-year revenue,
> net margin, P/E multiple) plus a sanity check via asset/replacement. Give a range.
