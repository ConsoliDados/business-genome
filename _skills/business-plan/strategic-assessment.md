# Skill — strategic-assessment

> Chapter of [[README|business-plan]]. **CLI:** run for the assessment chapter. **Web-chat:** paste.

**Writes:** the *Strategic assessment* section of `…/strategy/business-plan.md`

## Steps
1. **SWOT** — strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats (specific, not generic).
2. **Porter's Five Forces** — substitutes, new entrants, buyer power, supplier power, rivalry;
   rate each High/Med/Low with a one-line why.
3. **BCG matrix** — place each product line: cash cow / star / question mark / dog.

## Guard-rails
- Tie findings back to the market study; each force/quadrant needs a concrete justification.

---
## Prompt (copy-paste)
> Do the strategic assessment for <product/portfolio>: a specific SWOT, Porter's Five Forces
> (rate each H/M/L with why), and a BCG placement of the product lines. Ground it in the market
