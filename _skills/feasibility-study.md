# Skill — feasibility-study

> **CLI:** run to fill `strategy/feasibility.md`. **Web-chat:** paste the prompt.

**Writes:** `…/strategy/feasibility.md`

## Steps
1. **Recommendation up front:** GO / NO-GO / CONDITIONAL + key drivers.
2. Scope & assumptions (tag H/M/L confidence) + constraints.
3. Four feasibility dimensions: **market/demand** (→ [[market-study]]), **technical**,
   **operational**, **economic/financial**.
4. **Viability indicators** (→ [[finance]]): break-even · profitability · ROI/rentability ·
   payback · (NPV/IRR multi-year).
5. Scenario analysis (pessimistic/base/optimistic + sensitivity).
6. Strategic assessment (SWOT · Porter · BCG) + risk register.
7. Go/no-go decision: scorecard per dimension + verdict & conditions.

## Guard-rails
- Mark unknowns ASK-OWNER/ASK-CLIENT. Don't fabricate viability — state confidence.

---
## Prompt (copy-paste)
> Assess feasibility of <idea/project> across market, technical, operational and economic
> dimensions; include viability indicators (break-even, profitability, ROI, payback), a
> pessimistic/base/optimistic scenario, SWOT/Porter, a risk register, and a clear GO/NO-GO/
> CONDITIONAL recommendation with the conditions to proceed.
