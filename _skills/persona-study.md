# Skill — persona-study

> **CLI:** run to fill `strategy/personas.md`. **Web-chat:** paste the prompt.
> Model: persona triplet **Roles / Departments / Characteristics** + sales ICP/qualification.

**Writes:** `…/strategy/personas.md`

## Steps
1. **ICP fit gate** first (firmographic: size/sector/maturity + disqualifiers).
2. Per persona: role & **department** + **decision power** (decision-maker | influencer |
   gatekeeper); goals; **pains** (ask "main pain?" then "what else?"); jobs-to-be-done;
   **buying triggers/criteria** (cost/deadlines/value/security/brand/service); objections;
   where they are; **how we win** (value prop ↔ pains, proof/cases, qualifying questions).
3. Mark the primary persona; group personas under their segment.

## Guard-rails
- Base on real signals (interviews, the brief, sales calls), not stereotypes. Mark gaps ASK-*.

---
## Prompt (copy-paste)
> Define the personas for <product>: start with the ICP fit gate, then for each persona give
> role/department/decision-power, goals, pains (probe "what else?"), jobs-to-be-done, buying
> triggers & criteria, objections, where they are, and how we win them. Mark the primary one.
