# Skill — promote-post

> **CLI:** run when a published post earned audience and we want to go deeper.
> **Web-chat:** paste the prompt.

**Reads:** published `editorial/posts/<slug>.md` (+ its `metrics`) · **Writes:** a deeper piece
(usually Substack/blog), `derived-from` the original.

## Steps
1. Record the original's `metrics` (what did well, audience signal).
2. Draft the deeper piece (Substack newsletter or company blog) expanding the angle that landed.
3. Link `derived-from` and the `series`; cross-promote back.

## Guard-rails
- Promote based on real audience signal, not a hunch. Capture the metric that justified it.

---
## Prompt (copy-paste)
> This <channel> post did well (metrics: <…>). Draft a deeper Substack/blog piece that expands
> the angle that resonated, linked as a follow-up to the original.
